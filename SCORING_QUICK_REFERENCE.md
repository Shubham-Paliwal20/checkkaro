# Awareness Score - Quick Reference

## Formula

```
Final Score = 100 - (Basic Penalties + Additional Penalties)
Minimum: 0, Maximum: 100
```

---

## Basic Penalties (Per Ingredient)

| Classification | Points Deducted |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Generally Recognised | **-0** |
| Worth Knowing | **-8** |
| Commonly Questioned | **-20** |

---

## Additional Penalties (Per Ingredient, Cumulative)

| Condition | Points |
|-----------|--------|
| Banned in EU | **-15** |
| Banned in Canada | **-15** |
| Restricted by WHO | **-12** |
| FSSAI concentration limits | **-8** |
| Skin sensitization/allergies | **-8** |
| Endocrine disruptor concerns | **-15** |
| Carcinogenicity studies | **-20** |
| Banned in other countries | **-10** |

---

## Score Ranges

| Score | Verdict | Recommendation |
|-------|---------|----------------|
| **80-100** | Generally recognised ingredients | Can be used with general awareness |
| **60-79** | Worth knowing about some ingredients | Use with awareness of flagged ingredients |
| **40-59** | Several ingredients commonly questioned | Consider alternatives |
| **0-39** | Many ingredients subject to restrictions | Consider alternatives with fewer questioned ingredients |

---

## Example Calculation

**Product:** Dove Soap (Hypothetical)
**Ingredients:** 14 total

```
Starting: 100 points

Generally Recognised (7 ingredients): -0
Worth Knowing (3 ingredients): -8 × 3 = -24
Commonly Questioned (4 ingredients): -20 × 4 = -80

Additional Penalties:
- Skin sensitization (2): -8 × 2 = -16
- Banned in EU (1): -15
- Endocrine disruptors (2): -15 × 2 = -30
- Banned elsewhere (2): -10 × 2 = -20

Total: 100 - (24 + 80 + 16 + 15 + 30 + 20) = 100 - 185 = 0 (capped)

Final Score: 0
Verdict: "Many ingredients subject to restrictions"
```

---

## Key Points

✅ **Strict System** - Even minor concerns reduce score
✅ **Cumulative** - Multiple penalties can stack
✅ **Transparent** - Based on regulatory data
✅ **Educational** - For awareness, not health advice
