# CheckKaro User Guide

## How to Use CheckKaro

### 1. Open the App
- Frontend: http://localhost:5173
- Search for any product (e.g., "Maggi", "Lux", "Kurkure")

### 2. Understanding the Results Page

#### Top Section: Product Overview
- Product name, brand, category
- **Awareness Score** (0-100)
- Product image (if available)

#### Final Verdict Box
- **Color-coded** based on score:
  - 🟢 Green (80-100): Generally recognised ingredients
  - 🟡 Yellow (60-79): Worth knowing about some ingredients
  - 🟠 Orange (40-59): Several ingredients commonly questioned
  - 🔴 Red (0-39): Many ingredients subject to restrictions

- **Verdict**: What the score means
- **Recommendation**: Whether you can use the product

#### Complete Ingredients List
- Shows ALL ingredients in order
- Color-coded text:
  - **Red text** = Commonly Questioned
  - **Orange text** = Worth Knowing
  - **Black text** = Generally Recognised

#### Worth Knowing Section (Orange Box)
- Lists ingredients that are "worth knowing about"
- For each ingredient:
  - Name and aliases
  - Brief note
  - **Detailed effects** (why it's flagged, research findings, restrictions)
  - Regulatory information

#### Commonly Questioned Section (Red Box)
- Lists ingredients that are "commonly questioned"
- For each ingredient:
  - Name and aliases
  - Brief note
  - **Detailed effects** (why it's questioned, health concerns, restrictions)
  - Regulatory information

#### Generally Recognised Section (Green Box)
- Lists ingredients with no notable concerns
- Compact display (2 columns)

### 3. Score Interpretation Guide

| Score Range | Meaning | Can I Use It? |
|-------------|---------|---------------|
| **80-100** | Generally recognised ingredients | ✅ Yes, with general awareness |
| **70-79** | Worth knowing about some ingredients | ✅ Yes, be aware of flagged ingredients |
| **60-69** | Worth knowing about some ingredients | ⚠️ Use with awareness |
| **50-59** | Several ingredients commonly questioned | ⚠️ Use with caution, read details |
| **40-49** | Several ingredients commonly questioned | ⚠️ Consider alternatives |
| **0-39** | Many ingredients subject to restrictions | ❌ Consider alternatives strongly |

### 4. Example: Maggi (Score: 73)

**Verdict**: "Worth knowing about some ingredients"
**Recommendation**: "Use with awareness of flagged ingredients"

**What this means**:
- The product is FSSAI approved and can be used
- Some ingredients (like Palm Oil, MSG) are worth knowing about
- Read the detailed effects to understand what research says
- Make an informed decision based on your personal health needs

**Worth Knowing Ingredients**:
- **Palm Oil**: High in saturated fats, WHO recommends reducing intake
- **Monosodium Glutamate (MSG)**: Flavor enhancer, permitted but discussed in research
- **Disodium Inosinate**: Flavor enhancer, FSSAI sets limits

**Commonly Questioned Ingredients**:
- **TBHQ**: Preservative restricted in some countries, permitted in India under limits

### 5. Important Notes

#### What CheckKaro Does:
- ✅ Provides ingredient information for awareness
- ✅ Shows international regulatory data
- ✅ Explains why ingredients are flagged
- ✅ Helps you make informed decisions

#### What CheckKaro Does NOT Do:
- ❌ Does not certify products as "safe" or "unsafe"
- ❌ Does not provide medical advice
- ❌ Does not replace reading the actual product label
- ❌ Does not replace consulting healthcare professionals

### 6. Making Your Decision

Consider these factors:
1. **Your health conditions**: Some ingredients may affect certain conditions
2. **Frequency of use**: Occasional vs. daily consumption
3. **Quantity**: How much you consume
4. **Alternatives**: Are there better options available?
5. **Personal preferences**: Your comfort level with flagged ingredients

### 7. Tips for Using CheckKaro

1. **Read the detailed effects**: Don't just look at the score
2. **Check all sections**: Even "generally recognised" ingredients have notes
3. **Compare products**: Search multiple brands to compare
4. **Stay informed**: Regulations and research evolve
5. **Consult professionals**: For personal health decisions

### 8. Disclaimer

CheckKaro provides information for general awareness only. Our classifications are based on publicly available international regulatory data. We do not make health claims. Individual responses to ingredients vary. This is not medical advice. Always:
- Read the actual product label
- Consult qualified healthcare professionals
- Make decisions based on your personal health needs
